Hall Terms & Conditions

  • All events must close by 7:00 PM.
  • Live band and DJ must come with their own generator.
  • Each vendor is to pay ₦50,000 caution fee (refundable upon confirmation that no damage was done).
  • Ikeja Local Government fee of ₦20,000 must be paid before the event (collected on-site by local government officials).
  • Every event must obtain Safety Commission clearance. Events with above 250 guests must pay ₦31,500 to the Lagos State Safety Commission at least 48 hours before the event.
  • Decorators are allowed a day before the event to decorate (by 5:00 PM).
  • Cooking gas and fireworks are not allowed inside the halls.
  • 8 hours limit for hall usage.
  • Any damages caused by any vendor will result in the vendor bearing the cost.

Terms of Payment

To secure a booking, 50% of the total amount confirms the booking and must be paid into the company's account.

  • No cash payment.
  • All payments are non-refundable.
  • Payments via Cheque, POS, or Bank Transfer.
